Piece Pavilion Is Now on Instagram!

Piece Pavilion · March 11, 2026

We're excited to announce that Piece Pavilion is officially on Instagram! You can now follow us at @piecepavilion to stay in the loop on everything happening in our store.

What You'll See

Our Instagram is going to be the best place to catch new inventory as it drops, get a behind-the-scenes look at how we sort and list thousands of LEGO pieces, and see the custom builds we put together along the way. Here's what to expect:

  • New inventory highlights — Be the first to see sets, minifigures, and rare pieces as they hit our BrickLink store.
  • Custom builds — We love building original creations from our own stock. Follow along as we design and share new ones.
  • Behind the scenes — Sorting bins, packing orders, finding hidden gems in bulk lots — the day-to-day of running a LEGO resale business.
  • Tips and tricks — Quick LEGO tips, BrickLink advice, and building ideas in bite-sized posts and Reels.
  • Deals and announcements — Sales, restocks, and store updates before they go anywhere else.

Why Instagram?

The LEGO community on Instagram is huge. Builders, collectors, and AFOL (Adult Fans of LEGO) creators share incredible work every day, and we wanted to be part of that conversation. Instagram gives us a way to connect with you beyond just transactions — to share what we're working on, hear what you're building, and be part of the community we've always been fans of.

Plus, it's a great way for us to show off new arrivals in real time. Instead of just listing a set on BrickLink, we can give you a closer look at the details, the condition, and why we think it's worth picking up.

Follow Along

Head over to @piecepavilion on Instagram and hit follow. We're just getting started, and we'd love to build this community together. If you've got a LEGO account of your own, tag us in your builds — we'll be sharing our favorites.

And as always, you can shop our full inventory on our BrickLink store or reach out anytime at piecepavilion@gmail.com.
