Our Story

A family business built one brick at a time

Piece Pavilion started the way the best ideas do — with a kid who couldn't stop building. At 13 years old, our founder turned a passion for LEGO into something bigger: a real business, a real store, and a mission to connect builders with the pieces they need.

What started as one teenager sorting bricks in the basement and building their custom LEGO city quickly became a family effort. Parents, siblings, everyone got pulled in — not because they had to, but because the enthusiasm was impossible to resist. That energy is still at the heart of everything we do.

Today, Piece Pavilion is a family-owned and operated BrickLink store based in Massachusetts. We hand-inspect every piece, pack every order with care, and run the business the same way we started it — with passion, integrity, and a genuine love for the brick.

Whether you're hunting for a rare minifigure, finishing a set, or stocking up on parts for your next MOC, you're buying from a family that builds right alongside you.
